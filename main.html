PEACE TRAIN

2020 - 2024

Know More


OBJECTIVE : To explore the cultural gap between the western and eastern world


In the last few centuries, the population exchange between the two worlds led to a mass cultural collision. Some colonized, some vandalized, some remained in peace whereas some promoted the nuclear state of destruction. In the direction of the future, a motivated student group from University of Potsdam is willing to organize a cultural meetup between two different world orders. The project aims to connect different kinds of lifestyles around the world and to look at this diversity from a native perspective to rediscover the values and way of life. In order to initiate the first phase of the project, this coming spring, the student group is going to visit the Himalayan belt of Asia. Individuals who are interested to support the project can contribute their precious time or wealth and always welcome to join the group in any phase of the project.
