Who are We? Where are We? Why are We?

We’re, The Cosmopolitan Youth, the leading international political party at the Universität POTSDAM, with members from 17 nations. Our vision is to merge and make a global voice of youth, with a primary goal to form a globalized education system for the next generation.

Dear Students,

Every year in the month of June, Student election committee (StuPa) of the University Potsdam conducts an election among the registered lists to elect a council for Students parliament for that yearly session. The twenty-seven elected members of Parliament meets every 3 weeks for respective session.

The StuPa is electing the General Students Committee (AStA). AStA represents the interests of the students in front of the university. To do that AStA takes part in different commissions of the university senate, have a monthly meeting with the president and chancellor of the university. At present, AStA has 12 departments to represent all the important branches.
